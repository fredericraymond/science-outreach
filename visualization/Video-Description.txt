This video contains the visualization of sample SRS011134 from the Human Microbiome Project.
The DNA analyzed is genomic and is from a stool sample.

Sample: http://sra.dnanexus.com/samples/SRS011134

Software: http://denovoassembler.sourceforge.net/

The graph and assembled sequences were generated with Ray 2.3.0
